About This Tracker

This dashboard monitors four key indicators of silver market stress:

  • Lease Rates - High rates (>5-10%) indicate physical shortages
  • Physical Premiums - Wide premiums show retail/dealer supply tightness
  • COMEX Inventory - Drawdowns signal delivery demand exceeding supply
  • Margin Stability - Frequent hikes indicate exchange concern about volatility

When all four indicators normalize (green), the market is balanced. Red indicators signal stress.

Data updates every 12 hours via automated scripts

Sources: CME Group, Kitco, papervsphysical.com, Yahoo Finance
